Who is Sen. Kaka Shehu

Sen. Kaka Shehu Lawan was born in Borno State. He attended Bokolis collage of legal studies which is now known as Mohammed Goni Collage of legal studies, to further his education he attended university of Maiduguri where he studied Law.
He was Appointed As Commissioner for Justice and Attorney General of Borno State By the formal Governor who is now the Current Vice President of Nigeria Sen. Kashim Shattima Mustapha GCON
. After the end of Sen. Kashim Tenure, because of his Smartness and hardworking performance Governor Babagana Umara Zullum Re-appointed him as Commissioner for Justice and Environment. only that Kaka Shehu was appointed into 44 Committees of the Executive Council sect by His Excellency as either the Chairman or Secretary.
